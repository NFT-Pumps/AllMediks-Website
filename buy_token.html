Welcome to AllMediks Ecosystem

Our Mission

Connecting the Blockchain Technology and the Medical Industry.

AllMediks aim to create a global health care ecosystem, allowing you to access medical services from the comfort of your home. Creating a safer environment by providing medical consultation from highly experienced doctors and well trained medical professionals via our Web and Mobile App. This is truly a gift of time for all patients, doctors and nurses.

About AllMediks Token

AllMediks token is designed to be used as the main currency within the ecosystem, this will allow holders to benefit from the use of all services provided. Token holders will also benefit from a 10% commission for registration of any medical personnel/medical business referred by them. A further 10% will be secured and used to purchase tokens at strategic points, raising the floor price and maintaining a healthy chart. Persons who are in need of medical care and are unable to afford can also apply for medical services through our Medical Assistance Program.

You must be a holder of AMS token and you’ll be approved according to requirements met in each individual situation. In case you’ve missed the services being offered, please return to the main page and learn more about the benefits.
